About Sara

Art meets precision, on the most important day of your life.

Sara Valarezo is a Miami-based makeup artist with a passion for enhancing natural beauty and creating looks that feel effortless, confident, and timeless. With years of experience working with brides across South Florida, Sara brings a calm, professional presence to every morning-of experience.

From soft, romantic bridal looks to bold editorial moments, Sara's work is rooted in meticulous technique and a deep understanding of what makes each client feel their most beautiful.

Based in Miami and available for destination bookings worldwide.
